About San-Kernel

San-Kernel is a customized android kernel designed to enhance performance and usability for various devices. Our goal is to provide a robust and flexible kernel that meets the needs of users alike.

Features

  • Optimized performance for various workloads
  • Custom configurations for enhanced security
  • Regular updates and community support
  • Easy installation and configuration
